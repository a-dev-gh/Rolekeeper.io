📋 HOW IT WORKS

Four steps.
Same structure, every session.

Consistency is the skill. The structure makes it automatic.

1
MISSION
Write what you need. Add context, links, or examples — whatever makes the task clear.
2
ROLE
Pick who handles it. Each role brings its own identity and a library of prompts built for that perspective.
3
QUEST
Choose a template, fill the blanks, copy the assembled prompt. Works with any AI you already use.
4
LOG
Paste the output, rate it, add notes. Earn XP. Your sessions build into a searchable record of what works.
